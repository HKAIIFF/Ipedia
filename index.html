Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AIF Token and the private sale

What is AIF Token? +
AIF Token is the native utility token of the Hong Kong AI International Film Festival ecosystem. It powers a revolutionary platform for AI-native content creation, financing, distribution, and consumption.
How does the private sale work? +
The private sale consists of 25 rounds. Round 1 is currently open with a $3M target. Tokens are priced at $0.05 with a minimum investment of $10,000. Tokens have a 6-month cliff followed by 24-month linear vesting.
What are the token utilities? +
AIF Token has multiple utilities including platform access & payments, governance & voting, staking & rewards, and investment & trading across the ecosystem's six integrated platforms.
Who can participate in the private sale? +
The private sale is open to accredited investors only. You'll need to verify your accreditation status through our investor portal before participating.
What is the total token supply? +
The total supply is fixed at 10 billion AIF tokens. There will be no additional minting. Deflationary mechanisms include token burns from platform fees.
